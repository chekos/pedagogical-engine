# Interview Checklist

Use this checklist to track what information has been captured during the educator
interview. All required fields must be populated before proceeding to composition.

---

## Required Fields

### 1. Topic
- [ ] What the educator wants to teach
- [ ] Specific skills or concepts to cover
- Probe: "What specific skills do you want students to walk away with?"
- Probe: "Is this an introduction or are you building on something they've done before?"

### 2. Audience
- [ ] Who the learners are (age, background, role)
- [ ] How many learners
- [ ] Whether this is a new or existing group
- Probe: "Tell me about your students — who are they?"
- Probe: "How many will be in the session?"
- Probe: "Have you worked with this group before?"

### 3. Prior knowledge
- [ ] What learners already know (general level)
- [ ] Known skill gaps or struggles
- [ ] Whether formal assessment has been done
- Probe: "What do your students already know about this topic?"
- Probe: "Are there prerequisite skills you're confident they have?"
- Probe: "Any common struggles you've noticed?"

### 4. Setting
- [ ] Physical or virtual environment
- [ ] Room setup (lab, lecture hall, outdoor, home)
- [ ] Available equipment (projector, whiteboard, screens)
- Probe: "Where will you be teaching this?"
- Probe: "What does the space look like — do students have desks, screens?"

### 5. Duration
- [ ] Total session time
- [ ] Whether there are breaks or it's continuous
- [ ] Whether this is a single session or part of a series
- Probe: "How much time do you have?"
- Probe: "Is this a standalone session or part of a series?"

### 6. Tools & connectivity
- [ ] Hardware students will have (laptop, tablet, phone, none)
- [ ] Software requirements (Python, Jupyter, specific libraries)
- [ ] Internet connectivity (reliable, intermittent, none)
- [ ] Paid subscriptions or accounts needed
- Probe: "Will students have laptops or devices?"
- Probe: "Do they need any software installed beforehand?"
- Probe: "Is internet access reliable where you'll be?"

### 7. Goals
- [ ] What success looks like for the educator
- [ ] Specific outcomes or deliverables expected
- [ ] Bloom's taxonomy level the educator is targeting (even if they don't name it)
- Probe: "What does success look like at the end of this session?"
- Probe: "Should students be able to do something new, or understand something deeper?"

### 8. Constraints
- [ ] Budget or cost limitations
- [ ] Accessibility needs (visual, auditory, motor, cognitive)
- [ ] Language considerations
- [ ] Cultural or institutional requirements
- [ ] Anything else that limits options
- Probe: "Any accessibility needs I should account for?"
- Probe: "Are there any cost or licensing constraints?"
- Probe: "Anything else that might affect how I plan this?"

---

## Summary template

Once all fields are captured, confirm with the educator:

> Here's what I have:
> - **Topic:** {topic}
> - **Audience:** {audience_count} {audience_description}
> - **Prior knowledge:** {prior_knowledge}
> - **Setting:** {setting}
> - **Duration:** {duration}
> - **Tools:** {tools_and_connectivity}
> - **Goals:** {goals}
> - **Constraints:** {constraints}
>
> Does this look right? Anything I should add or change?
