## Lesson Context

This is a post-field-trip follow-up assessment for third graders (ages 8-9) who went on a 50-minute trail walk at Tilden Nature Area with naturalist Sergio. During the walk, they visited four plants (bay laurel, mugwort, soap root, oak/acorns) and learned about Ohlone ethnobotany and stewardship. The closing at Jewel Lake covered Sogorea Te' Land Trust and Cafe Ohlone as contemporary Ohlone examples, and students did a partner share about what they can personally do to take care of nature.
